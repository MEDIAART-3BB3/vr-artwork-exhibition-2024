Chinese Architecture In The Future by Yunuo Jiang

I added two models in the work that can represent Chinese elements very much; one is the Great Wall, and the other is a stone lion. The stone lions are located on opposite sides of the Great Wall, strictly following the position of the Chinese stone lions, and they are both traditions and culture that have been preserved in China for a long time. So, I think this history will not change and will continue to be passed on with time.
